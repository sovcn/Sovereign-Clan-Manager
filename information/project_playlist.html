Project Playlist Tutorial - Find Playlist ID


Step 1 - Register to website

Before you can put a music player on your site profile, you must first register on a website called Project Playlist. This website allows you to find music files on the internet and organize them into a playlist. To register you must first click on the link above and follow the directions posted below.

Once you load the Project Playlist website, you will be greeted with a homepage similar to the one above. You must click the link, as directed by the arrow, that says "Sign Up."

You will be taken to a page with a form on it that will allow you to register to the Project Playlist website. Fill out all of the required information to proceed.


Step 2 - Log in and add music

Now that you have registered an account, you will be prompted to log in. Do so.

Once you have logged in, you will be taken to your profile page. This is your main page where you will be able to access all of your information. Now you will be able to create a playlist and add music to your account.

Enter a song title or an artist's name that you want to add to your music player as directed by the red arrow. Once you have entered something, click "Search."

When the page loads, you will be presented with a list of results to your search. Next to each result you will see two buttosn, a "Play" button and an "Add" button. When you find the song you want to add, click the "Play" button to make sure that it works. If it does work, click that "Add" button. If this is your first song, it will ask you if you want to create a new playlist. You do, so proceed as directed. Then you will come to a page that says the song has been successfully added to your playlist. Repeat these steps to add as many songs as you want and then continue with the tutorial to learn how to add it to the clan site.


Step 3 - Add music player to your profile

At this point, you should have a Project Playlist account, and have some songs added to your playlist. Now you are ready to put your playlist in your clan profile. To being, navigate to your Project Playlist account page.

Click the link that says "View All" below your your Project Playlist profile picture. This will take you to a page with all of your playlists in it. Click the name of the one you want. You will be taken to a page with that playlist featured. It is the URL address we are interested in at this point, so take a look...

There will be a number at the end of the URL address. This is your playlist id. To add the playlist to your profile, go to your console. In "User Console" there is a link called "Edit Music Settings." Click it, and fill out the required information using that number as your Playlist ID.
